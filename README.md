## Summary
TradeMind AI is an AI-powered assistant that helps traders make better decisions by analyzing market risk, trading patterns, and behavioral factors. The system focuses on decision support and risk awareness instead of predicting guaranteed profits.

---

## Background
Financial markets are complex and highly emotional environments. Many traders lose money due to poor risk management and psychological bias rather than lack of information.

**Problems addressed:**
- Emotional trading decisions
- Poor risk control
- Information overload
- Lack of analytical support for beginners

This project explores how AI can act as a **decision assistant** rather than a prediction machine.

---

## How is it used?
A trader inputs:
- Asset (crypto, forex, or stocks)
- Entry price
- Stop loss
- Risk tolerance

The AI evaluates:
- Volatility level
- Historical behavior patterns
- Potential risk exposure

The system outputs:
- Risk score
- Warning signals
- Decision insights

## Challenges
Markets cannot be predicted with certainty. Unexpected global events and human psychology limit prediction accuracy.

**Ethical considerations:**
- Avoid misleading profit expectations
- Promote responsible trading behavior
